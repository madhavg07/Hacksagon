HACKSAGON-2020

Process & Eligibility Criteria

  • HACKSAGON-2020 is based on Hardware and Hardware in combination with Software category.
  • All UG/PG/PhD students from AICTE/UGC/IIT/NIT/IIIT and all Government approved educational institutions.
  • Team of maximum 4 students and a minimum of 1 student are eligible for participation.
  • No registration fees for first and second round.
  • Registration fees for third/Final round = Rs. 2500/- per team for non IEEE Members and Rs. 2000/- for IEEE members (If any member of the team is IEEE member then that team is eligible for this discount) or Teams from organizing institutes (ABV-IIITM Gwalior, IIIT Una, IIIT Ranchi, IIIT Nagpur, IIIT Lucknow, IIITDM Kurnool).
  • Registration Fees include Registration Kit, Stay and Food Expenses for the Final Round.
  • One member from Team must be physically present for the second round at selected regional centers.
  • For Registration & more details regarding HACKSAGON-2020, please visit at: https://ieee.iiitm.ac.in

Level 1

  • Online Submission of Title, Category & Abstract of Project
  • Last date of Submission & Registration: 28th Feb. 2020.
  • Announcement of Result: 3rd March 2020.

Level 2

  • Presentation of Idea/Project (ppt format is given on website).
  • Last date of Round 2 Registration: 7th March 2020.
  • Teams have to select the regional center for 2nd round (20/03/2020-24/03/2020) in the registration form.
  • Announcement of Result: 25th March 2020.

Level 3

  • Final Round at ABV-IIITM Gwalior
  • Registration Deadline for Final round: 31st March 2020.
  • Final dates of third round will be announced after round 2.
  • Announcement of Results & Prize Distribution.
